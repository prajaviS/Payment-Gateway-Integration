Shiksha For a Cause

This organisation works directly for the underprivilaged children and works on providing them basic right of education.
Our team members works continously to provide education to children from backward caste and who lives in rural areas.
Quality education builds self-esteem, leadership and confidence, and it can help marginalised
women bring change in their families, schools and communities.

About US

Shiksha Education Program is playing an important role in education of Underprivilaged students and improving the whole education system. Our goal is to empower millions of Dalit and OBC children and provide them the basic right i.e. The Right To Education. Our Organisation has reached 124,879 children, including 71,554 girls, and 90,000 boys and helped them in becoming a part of the Indian education system. More than 3590 schools across 7 states are benefiting through our initiative.
